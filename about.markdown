I hate writing about myself. In fact I can't think of of less interesting topic for either myself or my reader (that's you). For myself, I am already well enough acquanted with myself that any time writing it out is wasted. As for you, well you know enough to have gotten here so that should be enough for now.  If you want to know more it should be a simple enough task. I use my real name and a single pseudoname (with minor variations due to username restrictions) that should be trivial to find. If you want to know anything my Twitter account is the best way to reach me. The email address below isn't my "real" email and I barely check it.

## What is this site for

This website is my own personal [Zettelkasten](). I have created it and maintain it to help myself remember what I learn and to serve as a reference for myself. I am publishing this publicly in the spirit of [learning in public](https://beth.typepad.com/beths_blog/2009/10/learning-in-public.html).

## The Technology

I am always learning new things but I have never found a notetaking app that really worked. Of them all [Notion](https://www.notion.so/) is the best and I really liked [Joplin](https://joplinapp.org/) as well. Still it wasn't quite what I've been looking for. Eventually I came to the conclusion that the best note app was no app at all. Now everything is just .md files within a [github repository](https://github.com/BillLudwig/Zettelkasten) and a simple [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/) blog hosted for free on [Netlify](https://www.netlify.com/).
