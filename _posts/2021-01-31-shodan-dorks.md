[Shodan](https://www.shodan.io/) is a specialized search engine for IoT devices connected to the internet.  There is also an [exploit search](https://exploits.shodan.io) that looks for CVE, Exploit DB and Metasploit exploits.

## Filters

Format: `filter:value` without a space. Uses quotations if the value has spaces. *city:"New York"* Filters can also be used to exclude by prepending the filter with a '-'. *-city:"New York"*

## CLI/API

Shodan provides a [CLI](https://cli.shodan.io/) and [API](https://developer.shdan.io/api) for programmatic access.
