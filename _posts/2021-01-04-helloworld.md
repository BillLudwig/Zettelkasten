When I woke up this morning my plan was to relax, do some light cybersecurity reading, and take some notes. I didn't expect that to somehow end up with me creating a personal website, abandoning my existing note apps (yes, plural), and adopting a whole new system, but here we are.

I wont bore you with the details but today I realized that I wanted my notes to be stored locally in simple text/markdown. While looking for an easy way to do this I discovered [Zettelkasten]({% post_url 2021-01-04-zettelkasten %}) and I really like the technique for keeping my notes organized and useful.

I'll be using this website as a digital repository for all of the different things that I am interested in. I don't know if it will ever be useful for anybody else but honestly that is a secondary consideration.

So here is the obligitory `Hello World` post. Guess it's time to start moving my notes over here.
