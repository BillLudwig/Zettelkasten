[Metasploit](https://www.metasploit.com/) is an [open source](https://github.com/rapid7/metasploit-framework) tool for pentesting. There is both a paid and free version but the free version should be more than good enough for learning on.

Metasploit can be launched either through the `applications > Frameworks` dropdown in Kali linux or via the `msfconsole` command.
