The [Common Weakness Enumeration](https://cwe.mitre.org/index.html) is a community-developed system for categorizing software vulnerabilities and weaknesses. It is broken down into three main sections; [Software Development](https://cwe.mitre.org/data/definitions/699.html), [Hardware Design](https://cwe.mitre.org/data/definitions/1194.html), and [Research Concepts](https://cwe.mitre.org/data/definitions/1000.html). The current version (3.2) was released in January 2019.
