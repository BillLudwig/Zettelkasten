So I got an API key for `peopledatalabs.com` to see what they had on me. Overall it was pretty much exactly what I expected. They found all my social networks which would be really easy since I use my real name as my username whenever possible. They got my work, education, and address history mostly correct. Again not very difficult since all of this used to be on my public linkedin profile. There were some errors and they didn't have my current employer or address at all.
