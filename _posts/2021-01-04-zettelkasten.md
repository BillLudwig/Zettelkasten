Zettelkasten, German for "slip box", is a method of knowledge management where individual thoughts are captured on cards. These cards are then tagged and cross-referenced in order to reinforce the associations. The focus is on recording thoughts and knowledge in an organic way that best helps future you. For this reason each Zettelkasten is unique and personal to its owner.

I really just encountered the concept today but more can be learned at [zettelkasten.de](https://zettelkasten.de/posts/overview/) or you can watch the below video. At the end of the day Zettelkasten is a pretty neat idea and I'll take it as a starting place to organize the stuff I research. We will see how well I stick with it over time.
