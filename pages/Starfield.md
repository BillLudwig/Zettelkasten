- ## Weapons and Armor
  collapsed:: true
	- Tier is roughly based on character level
		- Base > Calibrated > Refined > Advanced
	- Rarity just adds traits which doesn't guarantee a better weapon
		- Common = 0 > Rare = 1 > Epic = 2 > Legendary = 3
